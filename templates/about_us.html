People Behind the Scene

Hi, I’m Andrew Yang, a Taiwanese high school student. I utilize my skills and knowledge to try to create social impact and spread positive thoughts. During this process, I found the plethora of thoughts that young students have, yet there is barely any method for them to be heard. With Arphostotle, we hope to let the public hear the voice of students and other groups suffering the same problem of not being heard, and create a community of care and empathy!

Hey, I’m Avinash Gu. As a high school student, I make a positive impact and spread positive ideas among fellow high schoolers through art. Along the way, I realize how influential the voice of youth can be if done the right way. With Arphostotle, we hope through design and writing, we can find the most extraordinary stories out of the most common people, amplify them through our platform, and create a chance for all to be a part of a social movement.
